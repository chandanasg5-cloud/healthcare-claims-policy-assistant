# Policy COV-001: Preventive Care Coverage

## Scope
Preventive services are covered at 100 percent when delivered by an in-network
provider and billed with an approved preventive procedure code.

## Rules
- COV-001.1: Annual wellness visits (procedure 99385, 99386, 99395, 99396) are
  covered once per calendar year per member.
- COV-001.2: A second wellness visit in the same calendar year is denied under
  denial code D-DUP (duplicate service).
- COV-001.3: Preventive services billed by an out-of-network provider are denied
  under denial code D-OON (out of network) unless prior authorization exists.

## Appeals
A denial under COV-001.2 may be appealed with documentation showing the visits
served distinct clinical purposes. A denial under COV-001.3 may be appealed with
a referral or proof of prior authorization.
